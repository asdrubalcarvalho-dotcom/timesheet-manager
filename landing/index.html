Core features

Timesheets

Accurate hours without spreadsheets. Technicians log work directly from mobile, managers approve in seconds.

  • Clock-in / clock-out per job or project
  • Mobile-friendly entry (phone & tablet)
  • Supervisor approval workflow
  • Export-ready for payroll & ERP

Expenses

Capture receipts in the moment, categorise and approve faster. No more lost paper.

  • Photo capture of receipts
  • Categories, cost centres & notes
  • Approval flow & reimbursement status
  • Export to accounting or ERP

Travels & km

Keep all trips, kilometres and per-diem in one structured place, ready for finance.

  • Trips with origin, destination & purpose
  • Km tracking & per-diem support
  • Link travels to projects or customers
  • Clear audit trail for inspections

Planning (Roadmap)

Visual scheduling for teams and jobs, connected to timesheets and travels.

  • Calendar view by technician & project
  • Drag-and-drop assignments
  • Capacity view per team
  • Roadmap: early access in upcoming releases

AI Assistant

Your co-pilot for daily operations. Ask in natural language, get clear answers.

  • Summarise weekly hours by client or project
  • Spot anomalies in overtime or expenses
  • Generate management summaries instantly
  • Available for both managers and technicians

Dashboards & multi-tenant

See your operations in real time, with secure isolation per company or business unit.

  • KPIs by team, project, customer & region
  • Multi-tenant by design
  • Role-based access (technicians, managers, admins)
  • Built to integrate with your existing tools
